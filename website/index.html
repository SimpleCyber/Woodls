Why professionals choose Woodls

01 Unmatched Accuracy

Woodls utilizes state-of-the-art AI models specifically trained on diverse accents and technical terminology. Whether you're writing code, legal documents, or medical reports, our engine understands the context and punctuates automatically, so you don't have to.

Stop correcting typos. Our intelligent correction system learns from your common edits, becoming more precise the more you use it.

02 Deep Integration

Unlike other dictation tools that limit you to their own text editor, Woodls lives in your system tray. It works globally across any application on Windows 10 and 11. Just point your cursor and start talking.

Dictate emails in Outlook, responses in Slack, or complex logic in VS Code. Woodls treats every input field like its native environment.

03 Privacy First

We believe your voice is private. Woodls offers local processing modes where your data never leaves your machine. For sensitive workflows, you can rest assured that your dictation is end-to-end encrypted.

04 Voice Commands

It's more than just speech-to-text. Use voice commands to navigate, delete entire paragraphs, or format text on the fly. Say "delete last sentence" or "new paragraph" to maintain your flow without touching the mouse.
